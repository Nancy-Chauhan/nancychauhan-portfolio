## Overview

Developers are overwhelmed by the volume of technical content published daily. Newsletters, blog posts, release announcements, and tutorials compete for attention, making it difficult to stay current without spending hours on content triage. SkillFeed solves this by acting as an intelligent content layer that understands what each developer cares about and delivers only what matters to them.

The platform goes beyond simple keyword matching by building a rich profile of each user's technical interests, current skill level, and growth objectives. When new content is ingested, AI models evaluate it along multiple dimensions — technical relevance, quality, novelty, and alignment with the user's learning trajectory. This means a frontend developer learning Rust will see beginner-friendly Rust content alongside advanced React articles, with the mix evolving as their skills develop.

Each delivered digest is designed to respect the developer's time. AI-generated summaries distill articles to their essential points, key takeaways are highlighted for quick scanning, and every recommendation comes with a brief explanation of why it was selected. The result is a daily or weekly briefing that takes minutes to consume but keeps developers genuinely informed about the technologies and trends that matter to their work and career growth.
